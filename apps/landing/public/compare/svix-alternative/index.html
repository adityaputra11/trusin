Looking for a self-hosted alternative to Svix?

Svix is a managed webhook sending service with customer-facing endpoint management. trusin is an open-source relay for teams that want to own the infrastructure for inbound provider webhooks and internal delivery operations.

Choose based on the delivery problem

QuestionSvixtrusin
Primary modelManaged webhook service for sending to customer endpoints.Open-source relay for receiving, routing, and observing webhook delivery.
Infrastructure ownershipService operated by Svix.Run with Postgres and Redis in infrastructure you control.
Best fitTeams shipping a customer-facing webhook product quickly.Teams operating internal integrations or needing a self-hosted delivery layer.
Operational trade-offLess infrastructure to run.More control, plus responsibility for deployment and operations.

When trusin is a good fit

Choose trusin when the event data plane needs to remain in your environment, when inbound third-party webhooks are the first priority, or when local development and terminal/MCP tooling matter to your workflow.
